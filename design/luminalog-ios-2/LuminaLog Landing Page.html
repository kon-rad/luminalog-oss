Questions

Everything you
might be wondering.

Is my journal private?

Yes. Entries are encrypted, transcription happens on your device, and we never train AI models on your journal. The whole app is open source, so you don't have to take our word for it — and you can delete everything, permanently, in one tap.

What happens to my voice and video recordings?

They're transcribed on your iPhone and stored encrypted. Only you can play them back.

Can it really read my handwriting?

Photograph your notebook page and LuminaLog converts it to text you can edit, search, and reflect on.

What does the AI actually know about me?

Only what you've written in your journal and your bio — and it only uses that to help you reflect.

Do I need a subscription?

No. Text journaling and daily prompts are free forever. Pro ($10/month or $100/year) unlocks voice, video, photos, and unlimited AI.

Which devices?

iPhone, iOS 17 and up. Sign in with Apple or Google.
