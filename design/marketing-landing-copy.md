## 1. Positioning

**One-liner:**
> LuminaLog is the journal that remembers — capture your life in writing, voice, video, or photos of your notebook, and talk to an AI companion that actually knows your story.

**Category:** AI-powered personal journaling app (iOS).

**Target audience:**
- Primary: reflective self-improvers (25–45) who journal inconsistently and want a habit that sticks.
- Secondary: voice-first people who think out loud and never liked typing; analog journalers with shelves of notebooks they can't search.

**Core differentiators:**
1. **Memory.** Every entry is indexed; the AI draws on your whole history, not just today's note.
2. **Any format.** Type it, say it, film it, or photograph the handwritten page — it all becomes one searchable journal.
3. **A companion, not a chatbot.** Chat or have a real voice conversation with an AI that knows your biography and your journals.
4. **Private by design.** Transcription and handwriting recognition happen on your iPhone; your journal is never used to train AI.

## 2. Brand Values

| Value | What it means | How it shows up |
|---|---|---|
| **Reflection over productivity** | This isn't a task app. No guilt, no metrics-first dashboards. | Warm copy, "streak" framed as a gentle flame, never shame for missed days. |
| **Memory** | Your past self is your best advisor. | "The journal that remembers" thread through all copy. |
| **Privacy** | A journal is the most private document a person owns. | On-device processing, encryption, no-training pledge stated plainly, not buried. |
| **Ease** | The blank page is the enemy. | Daily personalized prompt, voice capture, photo-your-notebook. |
| **Warmth** | Calm, human, lightly poetic — never clinical or hype-y. | Serif type for quotes, soft visuals, first-person copy. |

**Tone of voice:** warm, calm, sincere. Short sentences. Second person ("your story").
No growth-hacker superlatives ("revolutionary", "10x"), no therapy claims
(say "reflection" and "insight", never "treatment" or "mental health care").

### 4.6 FAQ

- **Is my journal private?** Yes. Entries are encrypted, transcription happens on your device, and we never train AI models on your journal. You can delete everything, permanently, in one tap.
- **What happens to my voice and video recordings?** They're transcribed on your iPhone and stored encrypted. Only you can play them back.
- **Can it really read my handwriting?** Photograph your notebook page and LuminaLog converts it to text you can edit, search, and reflect on.
- **What does the AI actually know about me?** Only what you've written in your journal and your bio — and it only uses that to help *you* reflect.
- **Do I need a subscription?** No — text journaling and daily prompts are free forever. Pro unlocks voice, video, photos, and unlimited AI.
- **Which devices?** iPhone (iOS 17+). Sign in with Apple or Google.

## 6. Claims Guardrails (legal/accuracy)

- Privacy claims above must match the shipped architecture (on-device STT/OCR, encrypted
  storage, no training, full deletion). If implementation changes, update copy first.
- Never claim or imply therapy, diagnosis, or mental-health treatment.
- "On your device" applies to transcription/OCR — AI insights and chat use secure cloud
  processing; phrase as "private, encrypted processing", not "everything on-device".
- Streak language stays positive; no loss-aversion push ("don't break your streak!" is out).
