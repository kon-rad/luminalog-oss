# LuminaLog — iOS App Icon Design Brief

## Brand Essence

**Name:** LuminaLog  
**Category:** Personal growth / Video journaling  
**Emotional positioning:** A private sanctuary for the examined life. Warm, introspective, expansive — not a productivity tracker, not a social network. A space where you see yourself clearly and grow.

**Core themes:**
- Light as metaphor (lumina = light, illumination, clarity of self-knowledge)
- The act of recording — capturing fleeting inner life before it fades
- Multi-dimensional growth: intellectual, spiritual, artistic, emotional
- Depth and interiority — what's beneath the surface

**Feeling the icon should evoke:** Like holding a candle in a quiet room. Like the feeling of finishing a long journal entry. Wonder at one's own inner world.

---

## iOS Icon Specs

- **Canvas:** 1024 × 1024 px (Apple requires this master; system applies rounded-rect mask)
- **Safe zone:** Keep all critical elements within a centered 820 × 820 px circle to avoid clipping at smaller sizes
- **No transparency** — iOS fills transparent pixels with black
- **Must read at:** 1024px, 512px, 180px (iPhone home), 60px (iPhone home @1x), 29px (Settings)
- **Format:** PNG, sRGB

---

## Design Philosophy

These are **logo marks first, app icons second.** Each concept should work as a vector shape in one color on any background — like X's letter, Discord's Wumpus, or Snapchat's ghost. Bold, heavy graphics only. No thin lines, no gradients unless intentional, no details that disappear at 60px.

---

## Concept Directions

### 1 — "The L" *(lettermark)*

A single custom-weight **L** letterform fills most of the canvas. Heavy, confident, slightly condensed — not a system font. The inner corner where the two strokes meet is the brightest point, as if light bends at exactly that angle. Warm cream on deep navy. *Ownable the same way X owns the letter.*

### 2 — "Sunrise" *(shape mark)*

A bold semicircle sits at the bottom third of the canvas, with **4 thick rectangular rays** rising from its flat edge toward the top. The rays are evenly spaced, same width as the semicircle stroke. Gold on deep navy. Reads "illumination" and "new clarity" at a glance. Strong silhouette at any size.

### 3 — "The Teardrop" *(shape mark)*

A single solid **teardrop** — wide at the bottom, tapering to a sharp point at the top — in warm gold, centered on dark charcoal. Suggests flame, a drop of thought, the shape of a journal entry. No outlines, just the fill. Owns a shape the same way Spotify owns the green circle.

### 4 — "Heavy Eye" *(symbol mark)*

An **almond eye** drawn with a very thick stroke (not delicate linework — heavy, like 80px weight at full size) with a large solid circle inside. Nearly fills the safe zone. Cream on near-black. Inspired by the CBS eye mark's directness — recognition in half a second.

### 5 — "Mirror L" *(monogram mark)*

Two **L letterforms mirrored** — one standard, one flipped horizontally — interlocking at their shared spine, forming a single symmetric geometric mark. Reads as both LL (LuminaLog) and as reflection/duality. Like a high-fashion monogram (Gucci, LV) but cleaner and bolder. Works in gold or cream.

### 6 — "The Dot" *(pure shape)*

Just a **large bold circle** — warm gold, centered, 700px diameter on a 1024px canvas. Nothing else. Extreme minimalism. Like a record button, a full moon, a sun through fog. Instantly distinctive because almost no app icon does this. Works printed on black, white, or dark backgrounds.

### 7 — "Four-Point Spark" *(symbol mark)*

A **4-pointed star** with elongated vertical points and slightly shorter horizontal points — the shape of a lens flare or compass rose, not a Christmas star. Bold, solid fill, no outline. Warm gold on deep navy. Conveys the instant of insight. Clean silhouette that reads at 16px.

### 8 — "Aperture" *(symbol mark)*

**Six bold blade shapes** arranged in a circle, alternating gold and dark, forming a camera iris/aperture. Graphic and heavy — not a technical diagram. Evokes recording and seeing at once. TikTok energy: a familiar object made abstract and bold enough to become a brand mark.

### 9 — "Arc + Dot" *(symbol mark)*

A **single thick arc** (the upper curve of an eye or a crescent) sitting in the upper half of the canvas, with a solid filled circle directly below it, slightly smaller. Two shapes. Reads as an eye, a horizon, or a face depending on the viewer. Very distinctive silhouette. Cream arc, gold dot, dark background.

### 10 — "Crescent + Inner Light" *(shape mark)*

A bold **crescent moon** shape in warm cream, with a small solid warm-gold circle sitting inside its curve — as if it's cradling a point of light. Evokes nighttime journaling, introspection, and the inner world held in darkness. Strong enough to be a brand mark; warm enough to be a journal.

---

## Typography (for brand use, not the icon)

- **Display:** Canela, Playfair Display, or Cormorant Garamond — literary, warm, confident
- **UI:** Inter or SF Pro — clean, legible at all sizes

---

## What to Avoid

- Anything that reads as "productivity app" (charts, checkmarks, clocks, grids)
- Social media aesthetics (hearts, thumbs, share arrows)
- Generic video-camera or play-button icons
- Overly bright, saturated, or garish color combinations
- Complexity that disappears below 60px
- Trendy gradients that will date quickly (Instagram-style multi-color rings)

## Evaluation Criteria

Rate each generated candidate against:

1. **Readability at 60px** — does the core motif survive?
2. **Uniqueness** — would it be confused with another major app?
3. **Emotional resonance** — does it feel like *your inner world*, not a tool?
4. **Dark/light shelf** — does it look premium on both dark and white iOS wallpapers?
5. **Brand alignment** — does it evoke light, growth, depth, and self-knowledge?
